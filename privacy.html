Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is dropX's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide the Service:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app (e.g., songs liked, genres followed) to improve your feed.
  • Account Data (Optional): If you create an account, we store your email and preferences securely to sync them across devices.

2. Guest Users

If you use dropX without an account, your preferences (followed artists, likes) are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is deleted if you uninstall the app.

3. Payment Information

We do not collect or store your credit card or billing information. All subscriptions and purchases are processed securely by Apple via the App Store.

4. Advertising

For users on the "dropX Basic" plan, we may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may use identifiers (like IDFA) to provide relevant ads, subject to your iOS privacy settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (such as Apple Music API) to provide music metadata. These services have their own privacy policies regarding data usage.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with the Service. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our servers.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@dropx.lat.
